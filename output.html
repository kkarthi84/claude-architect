## Around Your 90-Minute Morning Run

### **Before (30-60 min prior)**
- **Easily digestible carbs:** 200-300 kcal
  - Banana + 1 tbsp peanut butter
  - Or: toast with honey
  - Or: sports drink (200-300 ml)
- **Small amount of fluid:** 300-500 ml water

### **During (for 90+ minutes)**
- **Sports drink or fuel:** 30-60g carbs/hour
  - 500-1,000 ml isotonic sports drink, OR
  - Energy gels every 30-45 min
- **Water:** 150-250 ml every 15-20 min

### **After (within 30-60 min)**
- **Recovery window:** 40-80g carbs + 15-25g protein
  - Chocolate milk (500 ml)
  - Bagel + salmon
  - Oatmeal with Greek yogurt and berries
  - Pasta with chicken
